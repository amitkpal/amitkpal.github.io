Welcome to my homepage. I am Amit, working as an Associate Professor at the [Department of Physics](https://physics.iitpkd.ac.in), [IIT Palakkad](https://iitpkd.ac.in). My research brings together cocepts from quantum information theory,  quantum many-body physics, quantum optics, quantum thermodynamics, and open quantum systems. Some of my current focuses  include quantum information processing in low-dimensional quantum spin models, measurement-based quantum protocols and error correction/mitigation, non-equilibrium quantum systems and quantum thermodynamics, quantum devices and applications in presence of noise, and many-body noisy quantum metrology.
